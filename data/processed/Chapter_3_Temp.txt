вЮҐ
Regions with charged impurity atoms are referred to as the space charge region.
вЮҐ
Essentially all electrons and holes are swept out of the space charge region by the 
electric field. 
вЮҐ
Since the space charge region is depleted of any mobile charge, this region is also 
referred to as the Depletion region.
вЮҐ
Density gradients still exist in the majority carrier 
concentrations at each edge of the space charge 
region.
вЮҐ
Diffusion forces, acts on the electrons and holes at 
the edges of the space charge region.
вЮҐ
The electric field produces another force which is in 
the opposite direction to the diffusion forces.
вЮҐ
In thermal equilibrium, the diffusion force and the 
E-field force exactly balance each other.
