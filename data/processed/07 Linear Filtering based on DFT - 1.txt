• In frequency domain
• If y(n) is represented by spectrum Y(ω), the number of samples must 
be equal or exceed L+M-1
• Therefore, DFT of size N ≥ L+M-1 is required
• DFT of length N is increased to L+M-1 by zero padding
• Thus, DFT can be used for linear filtering
